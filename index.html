Guidelines to participate


Buy Tacos

1) After logging into your MetaMask account, navigate to our Exchange.
2) Scroll down to 'Buy Tacos' and enter the amount of ETH you wish to spend.
3) Click 'BUY' button.
4) MetaMask will then prompt you to authorize the transaction and assign GWEI (satoshi for ETH) for Gas (transaction speed fee).

Sell Tacos

1) After logging into your MetaMask account, navigate to our Exchange.
2) Scroll down to 'Sell Tacos' and enter the amount of ETH you wish to spend.
3) Click 'SELL' button.
4) MetaMask will then prompt you to authorize the transaction and assign GWEI (satoshi for ETH) for Gas (transaction speed fee).
5) Any ETH from selling is deposited into your Dividends.

Withdraw

This allows you to get all your ETH back to your account (not recommended if you are an early investor)
1) After logging into your MetaMask account, navigate to our Exchange.
2) Scroll down to 'Sell Tacos' and click the 'Withdraw Earnings' button.
3) MetaMask will then prompt you to authorize the transaction and assign GWEI (satoshi for ETH) for Gas (transaction speed fee).

Reinvest

This allows you to reinvest your current earnings into more Tacos, increasing your future earnings!
1) After logging into your MetaMask account, navigate to our Exchange.
2) Scroll down to 'Buy Tacos' and click the 'Reinvest Earnings' button.
3) MetaMask will then prompt you to authorize the transaction and assign GWEI (satoshi for ETH) for Gas (transaction speed fee).




How to install and configure MetaMask

1) Download MetaMask
2) Create your metamask account password and then store your 12 seed words.
3) Open MetaMask, click the '...' icon, and select 'Copy address to clipboard'.
4) Go where you currently have ETH (Binance, Bittrex, etc.) and find the 'Send/Transfer' feature for your wallet.
5) Transfer Ethereum to your MetaMmask address (previously copied to clipboard).
6) Once you have funds in Metamask, go to our Exchange to 'Buy/Sell' Tacos!
